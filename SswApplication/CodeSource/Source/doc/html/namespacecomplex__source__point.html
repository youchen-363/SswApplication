Computes the field radiated from a complex source point in 2D. Normalised by inputted radiated power and max gain.
Source: G.A. Deschamps, Gaussian beam as a bundle of complex rays, Electron. Lett. 7 (1971) 684â€“685, https://doi.org/10.1049/el:19710467.

Parameters
[in]GeomStructure that contains
.Nz = nb of points along z (vertical)
.z_step = computation step along z (vertical)
[in]cspStructure that contains
.k0 = wavenumber in rad/m (real)
.x_s = position of the csp along x (real <0) supposedly placed before x=0.
.z_s = position of the csp along z (real)
.W0 = width of the complex source point (real)
.P_Tx = radiated power in W (real)
.G_Tx = max gain in dBi (real)
[out]e_field(n_z)-complex array. Contains the field radiated by the csp. Max norm = corresponds to the inputted power and max gain.
