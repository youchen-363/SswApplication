Computes the field radiated from a complex source point. Normalised by inputted radiated power and max gain.

[in]geomStructure that contains

.z_step = computation step along z (vertical)
[in]CSPStructure that contains
.k0 = wavenumber in rad/m (real)

.y_s = position of the CSP along y (real)
.z_s = position of the CSP along z (real)

.P_Tx = radiated power in W (real)
.G_Tx = max gain in dBi (real)
[out]E-field(N_y,N_z)-complex array. Contains the field radiated by the CSP. Max norm = corresponds to the inputted power and max gain.
